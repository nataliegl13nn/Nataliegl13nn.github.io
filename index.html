CRAP Principles Analysis: AloYoga.com

Contrast

Alo Yoga uses high contrast throughout its site to direct attention. Large product photos with white backgrounds paired with black text and bold CTA buttons, helps users easily distinguish what’s clickable. Color contrast between headings and body text enhances scannability while maintaining a sleek, minimal style.

Example of strong contrast using large product photos and minimalistic color scheme.

Repetition

The site applies repetition through consistent font styles, button shapes, and layout structure across all product and content pages. This visual consistency reinforces the brand’s clean identity and makes navigation feel intuitive. Repetition is clear in the “Shop the Look” sections and repeated use of white space and muted tones.

Consistent product tile layout repeated across sections of the site.

Alignment

All elements on the site are well aligned from navigation bars to product grids. Text is left aligned for readability, and product tiles are evenly spaced in a structured grid, guiding the eye logically. Dropdown menus and headers align cleanly with the overall page design, giving the layout a polished look.

Navigation and category sections are aligned for clean browsing flow.

Proximity

Alo Yoga effectively groups related content together. Product descriptions are positioned directly beneath images, and related items are suggested below the product you’re viewing. This logical proximity reduces cognitive load and allows users to explore without feeling overwhelmed.

Product descriptions and pricing placed directly under related images.
